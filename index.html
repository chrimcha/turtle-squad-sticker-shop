Below: Our Story

The team has always wanted to expand out creations into the wonderful world of stickers. Though it is not something that is probable for us at the moment, we still felt eager to pursue designing stickers here and there.

Thanks to the game Sticky Business, we are able to live out our sticker shop dreams by creating a digital sticker business within the game.

And so Turtle Squad was born into a sticker shop that serves the digital Sticky Business community.

We hope you love what we love to create!
